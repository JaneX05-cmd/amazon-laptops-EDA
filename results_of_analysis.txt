The correlation analysis revealed no statistically significant effect of disk capacity on the final price of the device (Pearson’s correlation coefficient r = ~0.23). This conclusion is consistent with the current realities of the computer hardware market, where the costs of storage have become significantly commoditised. The main price-determining factors for this device segment remain: the amount of RAM, the processor class (CPU) and the presence of a dedicated graphics card (GPU). Ram showed high correlation and its mainly driving the price up in current times.
